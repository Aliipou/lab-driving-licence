Safety Guidelines

  • Wear safety goggles when operating cutting machines
  • Use appropriate gloves when handling chemicals
  • Never operate machines without lab supervisor presence
  • Maintain safe distance from operating equipment
  • Familiarize yourself with emergency stop buttons

Hygiene Protocols

  • Wash hands before and after using equipment
  • Wear masks when working with powder materials
  • Clean workstation before and after use
  • Dispose of waste in designated bins
  • Ensure proper ventilation during operation

Emergency Procedures

Emergency Contacts:

Fire Department: 911

Emergency Medical: 911

Lab Supervisor: 555-0123

Emergency Response Steps:

  1. Activate emergency power shutoff
  2. Evacuate the area immediately
  3. Contact lab supervisor
  4. Call emergency services if needed
