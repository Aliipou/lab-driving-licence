Safety Guidelines

Required Safety Equipment

  • Safety goggles must be worn at all times
  • Laboratory coat required for all experiments
  • Chemical-resistant gloves when handling substances
  • Closed-toe shoes required in lab area

Critical Safety Rules

  • No eating or drinking in the laboratory
  • No unauthorized experiments
  • Report all spills immediately
  • Know location of emergency shower and eyewash

Emergency Response Steps

  3. Contact lab supervisor
  4. If needed, evacuate following marked routes
  5. Meet at designated assembly point
